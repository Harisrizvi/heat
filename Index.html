Phase 01, Days 1 to 10
Wake the body up
Short easy sessions, 10 to 15 min. Build the habit, not the burn.
If anything feels too hard, cut reps in half. The goal this phase is just showing up every single day without fail.
Phase 02, Days 11 to 20
Build the base
Sessions grow to 20 min. Reps increase. Feel challenged but not destroyed.
By day 20 your stamina will be noticeably better. That is real progress even if the mirror has not changed yet.
Phase 03, Days 21 to 30
Push harder
25 to 30 min sessions. HIIT introduced. This is where belly fat starts moving.
Do not skip rest days this phase. Recovery is when your body actually burns fat and rebuilds muscle.
Phase 04, Days 31 to 40
Gym ready
30 to 40 min sessions. Full circuits. You walk into the gym already in shape.
By day 40 your endurance, core strength, and eating habits are transformed. The gym will feel natural, not intimidating.
